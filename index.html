Game Overview

Dustlands is an indie-developed survival game that takes place in a post-apocalyptic world.

Players must navigate a harsh environment filled with mutated creatures, scavenge for essential items, and build shelters to protect themselves. Along with survival mechanics, the game also offers an engaging quest system, tasking players with completing various objectives and missions.

With a focus on resource management, exploration, and combat, Dustlands provides a unique and challenging experience for fans of the survival genre.
